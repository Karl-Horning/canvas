Imperial Colours in Canvas

The colour information and accessibility ratings used on this page are based on Imperial College London's official brand guidance. The source material, including the Colour Accessibility Chart — Digital only, is available at Imperial's brand management platform (Imperial SSO required).

The tables below summarise which colours are suitable for use as text on a white background in Canvas content. They are grouped by their WCAG contrast rating when used as text on white:

  • AAA / AA — suitable for body text on white.
  • AA — suitable for large text, headings or UI elements on white.
  • Accent / background only — not recommended for text on white; best used for backgrounds, borders or highlights.

Using these colours in Canvas content

When applying these colours in Canvas, use them through inline styles on text or containers. For example: 

<p style="color: #0000cd;">
    This heading uses Imperial Blue as text on white.
</p>

<div style="background-color: #f5f5f5; padding: 1rem;">
    <p>This panel uses White Smoke as a light background.</p>
</div>

For combinations not shown here (for example, light text on a dark background), please refer directly to Imperial's Colour Accessibility Chart — Digital only or use a WCAG contrast checker to confirm that your chosen colours meet at least AA contrast.
